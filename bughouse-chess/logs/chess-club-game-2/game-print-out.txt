non-communicating, partnered AIs. What does this mean? It means that
this game will be played in a synchronous fashion, where, even though

an asynchronous version in the future. Additionally, each non-human
player is represented by an its own AI. And these AIs will not have
the advantage of communicating amongst themselves like you will.
